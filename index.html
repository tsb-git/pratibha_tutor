Pratibha Teachers bureau Since 2004

Pratibha has always been a pioneer in the field of education . Launched in the year of 2004 , it started its journey with a vision and a motto of providing an alternative employment to the teachers commiunity and producing brighter students 'Pratibha' is a proud organisation today with a strength of around 1200 dedicated tutors and morethe 3200 students under its umbrella .

We are still to achieve our motto of providing alternative employment to each and every educated persons living in the city of joy and culture . But , we believe that it is a journey beyond time and generations . 'Pratibha' will always work to uplift the cause of educational development of our city .
